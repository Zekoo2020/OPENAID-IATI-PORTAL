Insight into Dutch development aidopen data of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs

At this transparency portal you can find every development cooperation activity supported by the Dutch Ministry of Foreign Affairs since 2010. The portal enables you to find the activities in specific countries or regions, in sectors or by implementing organisation.

The data are reported as open data to the IATI registry and were last updated on 2015-12-09 10:00. The total number of projects is 5819.
